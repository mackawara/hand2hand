About Calyptic Computers

CALYPTIC computers is an established supplier of computer, printers and their consumables. We are the already the major supplier of IT hardware and consumables within the Hwange district. The Business was established in July 2014 and is already the premier purveyor of IT hardware and consumables for both the public sector and private sector Hwange. Existing clientele are schools, government departments and local business community
